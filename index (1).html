About This Project

I am Akhil, a student from Metpally, Telangana. I built Study Companion because many students in rural areas struggle to get textbooks or stable internet. This PWA caches books after first open, so you can study in zero network. It is free forever and will never charge. The Android APK version includes AdMob banner at top to support server costs, but ads never block study content.
